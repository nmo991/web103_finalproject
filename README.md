## About

### Description and Purpose

SkillSwap is a community-driven platform that connects people who want to learn new skills with those who are ready to teach them. Whether you're looking to master a language, learn an instrument, get fitness coaching, or develop a professional skill, SkillSwap makes it easy to find matches based on your interests and availability. Users can browse available skills, create exchange requests, and build meaningful connections through peer-to-peer skill sharing. The platform democratizes education by enabling knowledge exchange without gatekeepers or high entry barriers.

### Inspiration

We were inspired by the idea that everyone has something valuable to teach and something they want to learn. Traditional tutoring and course platforms are expensive and rigid. We wanted to create a space where learning is collaborative, free, and driven by passion rather than profit. The concept draws from time-banking communities and peer-to-peer learning movements, adapted for a modern web experience.
